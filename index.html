Worldlink's Core Customer Experience Challenges

High Cost of Human Agents ‚ñº

Operating a large, human-based call center is a major operational expenditure. Training, salaries, and infrastructure for agents contribute to escalating support costs, impacting profitability and scalability.

Delays in First Call Resolution (FCR) & Long Wait Times ‚ñº

Customers often experience frustratingly long wait times, particularly during peak hours, leading to dissatisfaction. Complex or repetitive Tier-1 queries frequently require multiple transfers or callbacks, extending resolution times.

Limited Multilingual and Vernacular Access ‚ñº

Despite Nepal's rich linguistic diversity, digital support is often limited to English and basic Nepali, alienating a significant portion of the customer base. This hinders broader market penetration and inclusive digital service delivery.

Service Inconsistency and Quality Variation ‚ñº

Reliance on individual human agents can lead to variability in service quality, tone, and the accuracy of information provided. Maintaining consistent service standards across a large human team presents a continuous operational challenge.
